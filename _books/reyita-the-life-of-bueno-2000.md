<div data-schema-version="9"><p>I am Reyita, a regular, ordinary person. A natural person, respectful, helpful, decent, affectionate and very independent.</p> <p>For my mother, it was an embarrassment, that I - of her four daughters - was the only black one. I always felt the difference between us, because she didn't have as much affection for me as she did for my sisters.</p> </div>
