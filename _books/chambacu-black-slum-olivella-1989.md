<div data-schema-version="9"><p>Kneeling before her son, she spoke softly, as if he had merely received a scratch from a cockspur: </p> <p>“My son, how many times I warned you!” </p> <p>Crossing the kitchen, Dominguito whispered to his aunt: </p> <p>“Camilo and the rest of the kids are waiting for us at Miss Domitila‘s school. I’ve got the avocado seeds.” </p> <p>With four sutures, the tobacco roller sewed Máximo‘s eyes closed. Many people in Chambacú now had theirs open.</p> </div>
